Is a one way taxi cheaper than a round trip?

For single-direction travel, yes — you are billed only for the distance driven, not a return leg the driver has to cover empty.

Do I need to pay driver batta for one way trips?

Driver allowance is built into the one-way fare we quote you, so there are no add-on charges at drop-off.

Can I book a one way taxi for late night travel?

Yes, our dispatch line is open 24/7, including for early-morning airport drops.
